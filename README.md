# AI Governance 101 — Free E-Learning Course

A self-paced (~2.5 hour) e-learning course on AI governance: global principles (OECD, UNESCO, India's 7 Sutras), major frameworks (NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, EU AI Act), frontier AI company practices, risk identification, pre-deployment evaluation, engineering controls, and production monitoring & escalation.

**Take the course:** open the GitHub Pages link for this repository (Settings → Pages), or simply download `index.html` and open it in any modern browser — the course is fully self-contained and works offline.

Features: voice narration (browser text-to-speech — best in Chrome/Edge), 6 interactive exercises, 4 knowledge checks, and a 15-question final quiz (70% to pass).
